About The Event

In 1933, a resolution of the Sabha Prathinidhi Mandalam led to the formation of Mar Thoma Yuvajana Sakhyam (MTYS), the youth organization of the Malankara Mar Thoma Church. MTYS functions as an approved body of the Malankara Mar Thoma Syrian Church with wings instituted in all continents, which strive to unite youths under one umbrella. The organization was formed with a vision to help the youths of the Mar Thoma church to accept Jesus Christ as their savior and Lord, and bear witness to His powers.

Rt. Rev. Dr. Isaac Mar Philoxenos Episcopa

Diocese of North America and Europe.

An inspiring leader and a great visionary. He got MTh Degree from Princeston Theological Seminary and Doctorate from Banaras Hindu University. It is significant that the university awarded Doctorate on the basis of research based on the place of grace in Vaishnava Philosophy and Christian Theology. He was elected as a member of the World Council of Churches (WCC) Central Committee for a second time and worked in WCC, Policy Reference Committee, WCC Reference Committee on Justice and Peace, Chairman of ECC and unit 1 of NCCI. He was elected a second time as President of Senate of Serampore.


Rev. Dr. Joseph Daniel

Currently serves as the Professor of Church History, Mar Thoma Theological Seminary, Kottayam. Post Doctoral (Habilitation) scholar, university of Bern, Switzerland. Mar Thoma Theological commission member Executive Committee Member, United Theological College, Bangalore. Member, European Society for Ecumenical Research, Germany Authored two books and several articles both in the national and international academic journals.
